02Typography

Three typefaces. Each has one job. Inter Tight for UI prose. Instrument Serif for rare editorial moments — marketing headlines and quoted voice. JetBrains Mono for every numeral, timestamp, domain, and log.

02.1
Typeface pairing
The three faces never blur into each other. A glance at a block of copy should tell the user whether they are reading prose, a number, or a marketing phrase.
